# Chicot's Usurpation - Buff List (Official)

This document contains the official list of Boss Blind debuffs and their corresponding usurped passive buffs for **Chicot's Usurpation** ($16 cost, Chicot + Luchador + Misprint), ordered as they appear on the Balatro Wiki.

---

## 1. Standard Boss Blinds (Ante 1 - 7)

| Order | Boss Blind | Debuff (Original Effect) | Passive Buff (Stolen Effect) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **1** | **The Hook** | Discards 2 random cards after playing a hand. | **The Hook**: Draw +1 card after playing a hand. |
| **2** | **The Ox** | Sets money to $0 if most played hand is played this round. | **The Ox**: Playing your most played poker hand doubles your money. |
| **3** | **The House** | First hand played this round is face down. | **The House**: First hand this round gives X2 Mult. |
| **4** | **The Wall** | Extra large Blind size (x4). | **The Wall**: Reduces Blind chip requirement by 25%. |
| **5** | **The Wheel** | 1 in 7 cards drawn face down. | **The Wheel**: Doubles all listed probabilities. |
| **6** | **The Arm** | Decrease level of played poker hand. | **The Arm**: Upgrades level of played poker hand. |
| **7** | **The Club** | All Club cards are debuffed. | **The Club**: Retrigger all played Club cards. |
| **8** | **The Fish** | Cards drawn face down after playing a hand. | **The Fish**: Cards drawn after playing a hand give X2 Mult when scored. |
| **9** | **The Psychic** | Must play 5 cards. | **The Psychic**: Played hands with 5 cards give X2 Mult. |
| **10** | **The Goad** | All Spade cards are debuffed. | **The Goad**: Retrigger all played Spade cards. |
| **11** | **The Water** | 0 discards. | **The Water**: +2 discards per round. |
| **12** | **The Window** | All Diamond cards are debuffed. | **The Window**: Retrigger all played Diamond cards. |
| **13** | **The Manacle** | -1 Hand Size. | **The Manacle**: +1 hand size. |
| **14** | **The Eye** | No repeat hand types this round. | **The Eye**: Playing a poker hand already played this round retriggers all scored cards. |
| **15** | **The Mouth** | Must play only 1 hand type this round. | **The Mouth**: Retrigger the first hand. |
| **16** | **The Plant** | All face cards are debuffed. | **The Plant**: Retrigger all played face cards. |
| **17** | **The Serpent** | After Play or Discard, always draw 3 cards. | **The Serpent**: Draw to full hand size after playing or discarding. |
| **18** | **The Pillar** | Cards played this Ante are debuffed. | **The Pillar**: Retrigger all cards previously played this Ante. |
| **19** | **The Needle** | Play only 1 hand. | **The Needle**: +1 hands per round. |
| **20** | **The Head** | All Heart cards are debuffed. | **The Head**: Retrigger all played Heart cards. |
| **21** | **The Tooth** | Lose $1 per card played. | **The Tooth**: Earn $1 for each scored card. |
| **22** | **The Flint** | Base Chips and Mult are halved. | **The Flint**: Double base Chips and Mult of played poker hands. |
| **23** | **The Mark** | All face cards are drawn face down. | **The Mark**: face cards give X2 Mult. |
